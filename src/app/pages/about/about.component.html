About me

My name is Marcello Munari, I live in Turin, Italy and I’m a travel photographer. Thanks to my passion for writing and adventurous journeys, I describe through words and images what I see, but most important what I feel. The later it gets, the more I realize there is too much to see on this planet to sit on my butt and not go out there and explore every corner! (although the Earth is round) Ever since I started traveling, I saw its power, I tasted that sensation of freedom, that feeling that pushes you to discover more , to see more and to learn more. This allowed me to grow and to expand my boundaries. I want to use photography and my stories as a means of communication, to ensure that people see right through my eyes and be inspired.
